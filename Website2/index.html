Missoula, Montana, USA

Location

Missoula, Montana is 475 driving miles west southwest of Seattle, Washington.

About

Weather

Typical temperatures range from 15 degrees Farenheit in December to 85 degrees in August.

Population

There are over 74,000 permanent residents of Missoula and additional 7,500 students at the University of Montana who live there seasonally.

Sports

The University of Montana Grizzlies compete in 14 NCAA varsity sports including basketball, football, soccer, and volleyball. You can watch the Missoula Osprey professional baseball team play at Allegiance Field in Ogren Park.

Things to Do

  • Fishing
  • Kayaking
  • River wave surfing
  • Downhill and cross country skiing
